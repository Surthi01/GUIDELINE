Introduction

The EZTest System is a secure, web-based platform designed to streamline legal and administrative workflows. It provides authorized users with a centralized space to manage case-related information, perform administrative tasks, and access essential legal data anytime, anywhere.

The system was developed with the goals of:

  • Efficiency: Simplifying processes such as case review, reporting, and document handling.
  • Accessibility: Enabling users to log in from any device with internet access, without additional software installation.
  • Security: Ensuring that sensitive legal and administrative data is protected with secure login and role-based access control.
  • Accuracy: Reducing errors by providing structured forms, standardized workflows, and automated updates.

Whether you are an administrator managing system users or a legal professional reviewing case details, the EZTest System is designed to improve your productivity while maintaining compliance and security.

Forgot Password Guide

If you forget your password, follow these steps to reset it:

  1. On the Login Page, click Forgot Password.
  2. You will be redirected to the Forgot Password page.
  3. Enter your Username in the field provided.
  4. Enter your registered Email address associated with your account.
  5. Click the Submit button (green) to proceed.
  6. An email will be sent to your registered email address with instructions on how to reset your password.
  7. Follow the link in the email and set a new password according to the Password Requirements.
